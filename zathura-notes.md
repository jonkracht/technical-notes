# zathura

## Shortcuts

* Vim-style for page motion:  hjkl is left/down/up/right
* Extrema:  gg is document top and G is document bottom
* (Capital) J/K is page up/down
* Capital N toggles status bar (bar along bottom of screen)
* Rotate 90 degrees:  CTRL + R
* Reload document:  R (shift + R)


* Toggle presentation mode:  F5


## Config

General form of mapping commands:
`map [mode] <binding> <shortcut function> <argument>`

where `mode` is one of normal, fullscreen, presentation or index (similar to modes in vim)
