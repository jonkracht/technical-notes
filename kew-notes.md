# kew (terminal music player)

https://github.com/ravachol/kew?tab=readme-ov-file

## Bindings

* Enter to play or enqueue/dequeue
* Play/pause  Space, p or right mouse
* +/-  To increase/decrease volume
* Show/hide playlist F2
* Show/hide library view F3
* F4 Track view
* F5 Search view
* F6 Show keybindings

* Cycle colors derived from kewrc, theme or track cover:  i
* Cycle color theme:  t

* Remove entry from playlist:  Delete
* Clear playlist:  Backspace

* Toggle visualizer:  v
* Toggle album covers:  b
* Toggle notifications:  n

* Switch to next view:  TAB

* Quit:  ESC
